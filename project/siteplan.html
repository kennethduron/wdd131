Scenarios


Automated Conveyor System:

A Siemens Logo PLC is used to automate the movement of a conveyor belt in an assembly line. It controls the starting, stopping, and speed adjustments based on inputs from proximity sensors and limit switches. CadeSimu is used to simulate the system, testing various scenarios like motor overloads or sensor failures to ensure the system operates without issues in the real-world setup.


Water Treatment Automation:

In a water treatment plant, Siemens Logo Soft controls the operation of pumps, valves, and sensors that monitor water quality and flow rates. CadeSimu is used during the design phase to simulate the plant's automation, allowing engineers to test how the system responds to changes in water flow or pressure and optimize the control program for reliability and efficiency before implementation.


Packaging System Automation:

A packaging line uses Logo PLC to manage the movement of products, filling, and sealing processes. Sensors detect product positions, and motors control the packaging materials, with the system ensuring correct operation. CadeSimu simulates the entire packaging process, helping engineers fine-tune the timing of each stage, ensuring optimal speed and efficiency without bottlenecks before deploying the system.
